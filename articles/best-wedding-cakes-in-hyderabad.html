Top Wedding Cake Styles in Hyderabad (2026)

1. Classic Ivory Fondant with Sugar Flowers

The timeless choice. Smooth ivory tiers, hand-piped sugar roses, peonies, or jasmine. Photographs beautifully in any lighting. Pricing: ₹12,999 – ₹35,999 for three-tier.

2. Buttercream with Fresh Flowers

Modern, relaxed look. Textured buttercream tiers decorated with real edible flowers. Best for daytime weddings and outdoor venues. Pricing: ₹9,999 – ₹24,999 for three-tier.

3. Gold Leaf and Pearl Couture

For maximum visual impact. Ivory base with hand-applied 24-carat edible gold leaf and pearl piping. Spectacular under wedding lighting. Pricing: ₹24,999 – ₹65,999.

4. Watercolour Hand-Painted Fondant

The 2026 trend. Hand-painted with edible food colours in soft watercolour patterns. Each cake genuinely unique. Pricing: ₹19,999 – ₹49,999.

5. Naked and Semi-Naked Cakes

Visible sponge layers between thin cream. Decorated with fresh berries, herbs, flowers. Boho and outdoor weddings. Pricing: ₹6,999 – ₹19,999.

6. Indian Fusion Wedding Cakes

Rasmalai sponge, Gulab Jamun layers, motichoor laddoo decor — wedding cakes that bridge Indian and Western traditions. Pricing: ₹11,999 – ₹29,999.

7. Rangoli & Henna Pattern Cakes

Fondant cakes with intricate rangoli or henna-inspired patterns piped in royal icing. Beautiful for traditional Indian ceremonies. Pricing: ₹14,999 – ₹39,999.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: Where can I find the best wedding cake bakery in Hyderabad?

Q2: How much does a wedding cake cost in Hyderabad?

At Krispie's: 2-tier from ₹4,999, 3-tier from ₹12,999, 4-tier from ₹24,999, couture from ₹49,999+. Pricing depends on tiers, decoration complexity, and flavour selection.
