Cake Delivery Options at Krispie's

1. Same-Day Delivery

Order ready-stock cakes before 11 AM for delivery the same day in a 4-6 hour window. Standard delivery fee: ₹49-₹150 depending on distance.

2. Express 1-Hour Delivery

Within 5 km of any outlet, we deliver ready-stock cakes within 1 hour. Available 10 AM – 8 PM. Premium fee: ₹150-₹350.

3. Scheduled Date Delivery

Choose your exact delivery date up to 30 days in advance. Free for orders over ₹1,000.

4. Midnight Surprise Delivery

For birthday surprises at the stroke of midnight. Delivery window: 11:30 PM – 12:30 AM. Premium fee: ₹250-₹500.

5. Multi-Address Delivery

Single order, multiple delivery addresses (great for client gifting). Coordinated by our account manager. Pricing on consultation.

6. Hotel & Event Delivery

Coordinated delivery to wedding venues, hotels, banquet halls. Includes on-site setup for multi-tier cakes.

How to Schedule Online Cake Delivery

  1. Visit www.krispies.in/menu and select your cake
  2. Click "Buy Now" on the product card
  3. Choose delivery date and time slot
  4. Enter delivery address and pin code
  5. Select payment method (UPI, Card, Cash on Delivery)
  6. Confirm order — you receive instant WhatsApp confirmation

Midnight Cake Delivery for Surprise Birthdays

One of our most popular services. Order before 8 PM for guaranteed midnight delivery the same night. Our delivery agent arrives between 11:30 PM and 12:30 AM. Premium fee covers night-time logistics.

How to plan a midnight birthday surprise

  • Order by 6 PM for guaranteed slot
  • Choose a flavour the birthday person loves
  • Add a custom message piped on the cake (no extra cost)
  • Provide gate/door instructions in the order note
  • Be ready with your phone for the doorbell at midnight

What to Do If Delivery Goes Wrong

Quality issues do happen occasionally. Here is how Krispie's handles them:

  1. Take photos of the cake immediately on receipt
  2. Call the outlet directly (not the delivery agent)
  3. We assess and either replace, partial-refund, or full-refund based on situation
  4. Our response time for delivery issues: under 60 minutes

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: Does Krispie's deliver cakes across all of Hyderabad?

Yes. Our 5 outlets cover the entire city — from Secunderabad in the north to Shamshabad in the south, Boduppal in the east to Mehdipatnam in the west. Specific area checker: contact page.

Q2: How long does same-day cake delivery take?

Standard same-day delivery: 4-6 hours from order. Express 1-hour delivery: under 60 minutes within 5 km of any outlet.

Q3: How much does cake delivery cost in Hyderabad?

Standard delivery: ₹49-₹150 depending on distance. Express 1-hour: ₹150-₹350. Midnight surprise: ₹250-₹500. Free delivery on orders over ₹1,000 in standard zones.

Q4: Can I get midnight cake delivery in Hyderabad?

Yes. Order before 8 PM for midnight delivery the same night. Delivery window: 11:30 PM – 12:30 AM. Premium fee applies.

Q5: How do I track my cake delivery order?

You receive WhatsApp notifications at confirmation, dispatch, and delivery. Our delivery agent calls when 10 minutes away.

Q6: Can I send a cake to Hyderabad from outside the city?

Yes. You can order online from anywhere in the world for delivery to a Hyderabad address. Payment via UPI, card, or international Visa/Mastercard.

Q7: What if the recipient is not at home during delivery?

We call the recipient 15 minutes before arrival. If not available, the cake is brought back to our outlet and a re-delivery is scheduled (additional fee may apply).

Q8: Does Krispie's deliver wedding cakes to event venues?

Yes. We deliver multi-tier wedding cakes to your venue (hotel, banquet hall, wedding garden) with on-site assembly included. Read wedding cake delivery guide.
