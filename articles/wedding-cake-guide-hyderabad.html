The Complete Wedding Cake Guide for Hyderabad Couples

Your wedding cake is one of the most photographed objects of your entire wedding day. It needs to be beautiful, delicious, and large enough to feed everyone. After making wedding cakes for over a thousand Hyderabad couples, here is what we have learned.

How Many Tiers Do You Need?

For Hyderabad weddings, the standard answer is three tiers — though this depends entirely on your guest count.

  • Intimate wedding (50-80 guests): 2 tiers (5kg cake total)
  • Mid-size wedding (100-200 guests): 3 tiers (7-9kg)
  • Large wedding (250-400 guests): 4 tiers (12-15kg)
  • Grand wedding (500+ guests): 5 or 6 tiers (20kg+) — often with additional sheet cakes hidden in the kitchen for cutting

How Many Slices Per Kg?

A 1 kg wedding cake yields roughly 10-12 finger-portion slices. This is smaller than dessert portions because guests typically eat the wedding cake after a full meal. Plan accordingly.

The Most Popular Wedding Cake Styles in Hyderabad

Ivory Fondant with Sugar Flowers

The classic, timeless choice. Smooth ivory fondant tiers, decorated with hand-piped sugar roses, peonies, or jasmine. This style has been popular for two decades and shows no sign of fading.

Buttercream with Fresh Flowers

A more relaxed, modern look. Soft buttercream finish (sometimes textured with a palette knife) decorated with real edible flowers like roses, dahlias, and gypsophila. Best for daytime weddings and outdoor venues.

Gold Leaf and Pearls

For couples who want maximum impact. White or ivory base with hand-applied 24-carat edible gold leaf and edible pearl piping. Spectacular under wedding lighting.

Watercolour Fondant

Trending hard in 2026. The fondant is hand-painted with edible food colours in soft watercolour patterns. Each cake is genuinely unique.

Naked and Semi-Naked Cakes

Sponge layers visible between thin layers of cream. Decorated with fresh berries, herbs, or flowers. Rustic, elegant, and increasingly popular for boho and outdoor weddings.

Wedding Cake Flavour Pairings

For multi-tier cakes, you can have different flavours per tier. This is highly recommended — it lets guests pick their favourite. Some pairings that work well:

The Classic Trio

  • Top tier: Vanilla with fresh cream (for the cake-cutting moment — it cuts cleanly)
  • Middle tier: Chocolate truffle
  • Bottom tier: Butterscotch praline

The Indian Trio

  • Top: Rasmalai
  • Middle: Gulab Jamun
  • Bottom: Pista-Badam

The Modern Trio

  • Top: Red Velvet with cream cheese
  • Middle: Lotus Biscoff
  • Bottom: Chocolate Hazelnut

Timeline: When to Book

For a wedding cake, longer notice is always better:

  • 3 months in advance: Ideal — locks in your design, allows for tasting sessions
  • 6 weeks in advance: Standard — works for most couples
  • 2 weeks in advance: Last minute but possible for standard designs
  • Less than 2 weeks: Limited design options, no custom sugar flowers

Tasting Sessions

Before finalising a wedding cake, we strongly recommend a tasting session. At Krispie's, tasting sessions include 5-6 small samples of different flavours, a consultation with our head decorator, and a discussion of design references. Tasting sessions are free if you place an order; otherwise charged at ₹2,500 per couple.

The Day-Of Logistics

Delivery Timing

We deliver wedding cakes 4-6 hours before the cutting ceremony to allow them to set up and reach room temperature. Cakes delivered too early can suffer in the air conditioning; too late and there is no buffer for traffic delays.

Setting Up

For multi-tier cakes, we send a decorator to assemble the cake on site. This is included in the price.

The Cake Table

Plan for a dedicated cake table in good lighting. The cake will be one of the most photographed parts of your wedding — it deserves a thoughtful setup. We recommend a draped table at a slight angle to the main dance floor, with soft uplighting.

Budgeting

Wedding cake pricing in Hyderabad varies enormously:

  • Basic buttercream wedding cake (5-7 kg): ₹8,000 - ₹15,000
  • Fondant cake with simple decoration (8-10 kg): ₹18,000 - ₹30,000
  • Premium fondant with sugar flowers (10-12 kg): ₹35,000 - ₹65,000
  • Couture wedding cake with gold leaf, pearls, hand-painted detail: ₹75,000 - ₹2,00,000+

What Could Go Wrong (And How to Avoid It)

  • Heat damage: Hyderabad summers are brutal. For May-June weddings, fondant is safer than buttercream
  • Travel damage: If your venue is far from any bakery, ensure the cake is transported in a refrigerated vehicle
  • Power outages: Have a backup plan with the venue's coordinator
  • Late delivery: Add a buffer of 2 hours to your delivery time
