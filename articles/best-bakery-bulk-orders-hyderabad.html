Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: Does Krispie's handle bulk corporate cake orders in Hyderabad?

Yes. We handle bulk orders from 50 units to 5,000+ units, with custom branding, dedicated account management, and GST invoicing. Email support@krispies.in.

Q2: What is the minimum quantity for corporate gift boxes?

50 boxes for our standard festive boxes. 25 boxes for luxury hampers. For custom-designed boxes, minimum 100 units due to printing setup costs.

For Diwali, order at least 14 days in advance. For Christmas, 10 days. Last week of October is peak season — book by early October for guaranteed slot.
