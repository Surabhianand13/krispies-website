The 10 Best Cheesecake Flavours at Krispie's

1. New York Baked Cheesecake (₹599 – ₹1,499)

The classic. Dense, rich, tangy. Graham cracker base. The standard against which all other cheesecakes are measured.

2. Blueberry Compote Cheesecake (₹649 – ₹1,599)

NY base with a swirl of fresh blueberry compote. Beautiful purple-blue marbling on top.

3. Lotus Biscoff Cheesecake (₹699 – ₹1,699)

The 2026 hit. Biscoff biscuit base + Biscoff cream cheese filling + caramelised Biscoff topping. Three-layer addiction.

4. Mango Cheesecake (₹699 – ₹1,699) [seasonal]

Alphonso mango puree blended into the cream cheese with a fresh mango glaze on top. Summer favourite.

5. Strawberry Cheesecake (₹649 – ₹1,599)

Fresh strawberry compote layer on top of NY cheesecake. Photogenic and refreshing.

6. Caramel Pecan Cheesecake (₹729 – ₹1,749)

NY base with caramelised pecans and salted caramel sauce. Rich, indulgent, premium.

7. Tiramisu Cheesecake (₹749 – ₹1,799)

Italian-inspired. Mascarpone cream cheese, espresso-soaked ladyfinger base, cocoa dusting on top. Sophisticated.

8. Rasmalai Cheesecake (₹729 – ₹1,749)

Indian fusion. Cream cheese flavoured with cardamom, saffron, and condensed milk. Topped with crushed pistachios and rose petals.

9. Red Velvet Cheesecake (₹699 – ₹1,699)

Red velvet sponge layer with cream cheese filling on top. Striking visual, ideal for Valentine's and anniversaries.

10. Japanese Cotton Cheesecake (₹599 – ₹1,499)

The lighter cousin. Airy, soufflé-like, jiggly texture. About one-third the calories of NY style.

How to Store Your Cheesecake at Home

Cheesecakes are best refrigerated immediately and kept covered. They typically stay fresh for 5-6 days when properly stored. Read our complete cake storage guide.

Quick storage tips

  • Refrigerate immediately in airtight container or covered tightly
  • Take out 15-20 minutes before serving for best texture
  • Cheesecakes also freeze well — wrap individual slices for up to 1 month
