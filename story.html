Our Story

From a single oven in Bangalore to five thriving stores across Hyderabad — a legacy baked in love.

"We didn't just open a bakery. We built a home — where every customer walks in as a stranger and leaves as family, carrying a little piece of our heart with them."

It began, as most great things do, with a family and a passion. In 1996, in the bustling lanes of Bangalore, a small Iyengar family opened the doors of what would become one of the most beloved bakeries in the region. There was no grand plan, no investors, no marketing campaign — only a deep, unwavering love for the craft of baking and a desire to share it with the world.

The recipes were handed down through generations, rooted in the time-honoured traditions of Iyengar baking — where patience is an ingredient, where every loaf is treated as a labour of devotion, and where quality is never negotiated. Soft, pillowy breads. Melt-in-your-mouth pastries. Biscuits so crisp and buttery that just the aroma could stop you in your tracks.

The Iyengar Baking Tradition

Iyengar bakeries are more than a business category — they are an institution in South Indian culture. Known for their purity, their freshness, and their uncompromising commitment to vegetarian baking, these establishments have fed generations of Indians with a kind of wholesome, honest food that is increasingly rare in the world of mass production.

At Krispie's, we carry this tradition with immense pride. Our team brings over 20 years of hands-on baking expertise — combining the reverence for traditional techniques with the creativity and innovation demanded by modern celebrations. The result is a product line that feels both timeless and fresh, both nostalgic and exciting.

1996
The Beginning — Bangalore

A family-run Iyengar bakery opens its first store in Bangalore. One oven, one team, and an infinite love for baking. The neighbourhood falls in love instantly.

2000s
Growing the Legacy

Word spreads. The bakery becomes a local institution — known for its breads, biscuits and sweets that taste like home. A loyal community forms around the store.

2010s
Expanding the Menu

Two decades of expertise allow the team to expand into customised cakes, celebration desserts, and modern pastry creations — without ever losing the soul of traditional baking.

2024
Reimagined as Krispie's — Hyderabad

A bold rebrand. A new city. A fresh chapter. Krispie's launches across Hyderabad with 5 outlets — Lalbazar, Suchitra, Boduppal, Ramantapur and Tukkuguda — bringing 30 years of baking mastery to a new community.

Why We Rebrand? The Story Behind the Name

In 2024, we made the decision to reimagine our legacy. The name "Krispie's" was chosen deliberately — it honours the crispness and freshness that has always defined our baking, while the apostrophe speaks to ownership and pride. This is ours. This is yours.

The iconic floral crest in our logo is a nod to the ornamental heritage of South Indian artistry — intricate, layered, and timeless — surrounding a bold K that stands for Krispie's, for Craft, and for the Community we serve. The champagne gold and deep black speak to the elegance of celebrations — because every moment with Krispie's should feel special.

What Drives Us Every Day

We believe that food has the power to create memories. A birthday cake is not just flour, sugar and eggs — it is the moment a child's eyes light up. A wedding cake is not just a centrepiece — it is the sweet punctuation on a lifelong promise. An anniversary cake is not just a dessert — it is gratitude made edible.

This is why every cake, every biscuit, every cheesecake, and every donut that leaves our kitchen is made with the same care we have always brought to our craft. We do not cut corners. We do not compromise on ingredients. We do not chase volume at the cost of quality.

We bake the way our founders baked in 1996 — with our hands, our hearts, and our heritage.
