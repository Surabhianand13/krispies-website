Our Story

From a Bangalore Kitchen to Hyderabad's Heart

Founded in 1996 in Bangalore as a small family-run Iyengar bakery, Krispie's began with a single store and a deep love for traditional baking. Our team brought over 20 years of expertise crafting soft breads, melt-in-the-mouth pastries, and iconic Iyengar-style treats.

In 2024, we reimagined our legacy and rebranded as Krispie's — a name that blends heritage with vision. With 5 thriving outlets across Hyderabad, we stand as a proud symbol of handcrafted excellence and community love.
