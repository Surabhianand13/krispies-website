Our Story

From a Bangalore Kitchen to Hyderabad's Heart

Founded in 1996 in Bangalore as a small family-run Iyengar bakery, Krispie's began with a single store and a deep love for traditional baking. Our team brought over 20 years of expertise crafting soft breads, melt-in-the-mouth pastries, and iconic Iyengar-style treats.

In 2024, we reimagined our legacy and rebranded as Krispie's — a name that blends heritage with vision. With 5 thriving outlets across Hyderabad, we stand as a proud symbol of handcrafted excellence and community love.

Krispie's — Best Cake Shop in Hyderabad Since 1996

With over 30 years of baking joy and being a part of lakhs of celebrations across Hyderabad, Krispie's is your trusted cake partner for every milestone. Whether it's a birthday, wedding, engagement, baby shower, or a corporate event, Krispie's has the perfect handcrafted cake and the most personal way to make your celebration unforgettable.

When it comes to reliable cake delivery to your doorstep anywhere across Hyderabad, Krispie's is the name trusted by over 3,000 happy customers. With 5 conveniently located outlets in Lalbazar, Suchitra, Boduppal, Ramantapur and Tukkuguda, ordering your dream cake has never been easier.

As Hyderabadis, we love celebrating every special occasion with great joy and warmth. Buying a beautifully crafted cake for a birthday, anniversary, or wedding is a heartfelt tradition — and Krispie's has been the most trusted name in custom cakes across Hyderabad for over three decades. Rooted in the warmth of Iyengar baking, every cake is made with the finest ingredients, fresh cream, and a generous serving of love.

We pride ourselves on being Hyderabad's one-stop bakery for every celebration — from a simple birthday cake for a child's first year to an elaborate 3-tier fondant cake for a grand wedding reception. Our team of expert bakers crafts each order by hand, ensuring that your cake is not just beautiful but truly delicious.

Browse through our cake collection spanning birthday cakes, wedding cakes, engagement cakes, baby shower and naming ceremony cakes, birthday theme cakes with fondant characters, and fully customised photo cakes. Our flavours include classic Black Forest, Chocolate Truffle, Red Velvet, Butterscotch, Rasmalai, Vanilla Dream, Pineapple, Mango, Strawberry, Biscoff, and many more — all available as eggless options on request.

Whether you are looking to order a cake online for same-day delivery in Hyderabad or walk into one of our 5 outlets, Krispie's ensures that your cake reaches you fresh, on time, and exactly as you imagined it. We also offer midnight cake delivery in select areas — because celebrations don't always happen at convenient hours!

For bulk and corporate orders — whether it's 50 cupcakes for an office party, a 500-piece dessert spread for a school annual day, or wedding favours for a large reception — Krispie's has the capacity and the expertise to deliver at scale without compromising on quality. Just WhatsApp us with your requirement and our team will get back to you within 10 minutes.

Krispie's custom cakes in Hyderabad are the talk of every celebration. Share a reference image, tell us your theme and flavour preference, and our bakers will craft a cake that perfectly matches your vision — at prices that are honest and transparent. No hidden charges, no compromises on freshness.

Why Krispie's is Hyderabad's Most Trusted Bakery

Krispie's has been baking since 1996 — long before "artisan cakes" became a trend. We started with the simplicity and purity of Iyengar baking traditions, and we've carried that philosophy into everything we make. 4.5+ star rating across outlets, 3,000+ happy customers, and 30 years of celebration memories — these are not just numbers, they are the trust Hyderabad has placed in us over generations. From Lalbazar to Tukkuguda, from birthday parties to grand weddings, Krispie's has been the quiet star of Hyderabad's most joyful moments.
