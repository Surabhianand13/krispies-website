Founder & Chief Dentist

Dr. Priya Mittal

MDS (Pediatric Dentistry) · FRCS Edinburgh · Invisalign Certified

Dr. Priya Mittal completed her MDS in Pediatric & Preventive Dentistry from AIIMS New Delhi — India's most prestigious medical institution. She followed that with a fellowship at the Royal College of Surgeons, Edinburgh, training under globally renowned pediatric dental specialists.

Her 15-year journey has transformed how dental care is perceived by children and parents alike. She pioneered anxiety-free dentistry techniques in the region, introduced digital X-ray and 3D scanning, and established one of India's first dedicated NRI pediatric dental concierge programs.

Outside the clinic, Dr. Mittal is a passionate advocate for school oral health programs, having personally conducted camps in 80+ schools across the NCR region.

Our Journey

15 Years of Growing Together

From a single-chair clinic to a full-spectrum pediatric dental centre — every milestone driven by one mission: healthier smiles for every child.

2009
Clinic Founded
Dr. Mittal opened the first 2-chair clinic in Gurgaon with a vision to make dental care fun and fear-free for children.
2013
Expanded to Full Centre
Moved to a 10-chair facility with dedicated play zone, digital X-ray suite, and separate paediatric waiting area.
2016
School Outreach Launched
Partnered with the first 10 schools to provide free dental screenings and fluoride programs — now at 80+ schools.
2019
NRI Concierge Program
Launched India's first dedicated NRI pediatric dental coordination service, now serving 500+ overseas families annually.
2024
10,000+ Kids & Counting
Crossed 12,000 patients served and won the India Healthcare Award for Best Pediatric Dental Clinic.
