Meet the Doctor

Dr. Priya Mittal

MDS | Pediatric & Preventive Dentist

With over 15 years of specialized experience in pediatric dentistry, Dr. Mittal has transformed more than 12,000 young smiles. Trained at AIIMS and with a fellowship from the Royal College of Surgeons in Edinburgh, she brings world-class expertise to every little patient who walks through our doors.

Her philosophy is simple: "A child who loves their dentist has healthy teeth for life." Every procedure is designed to be gentle, fun, and as painless as possible.
