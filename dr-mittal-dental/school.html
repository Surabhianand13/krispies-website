Why It Matters

70% of Dental Problems
Start Before Age 12

Most childhood dental disease is entirely preventable — yet it remains one of the most common chronic conditions in children. Early intervention through school programs changes this.

🦷
Early Detection Saves Smiles
Screenings catch cavities, bite issues, and gum disease before they become serious — and expensive — problems.
📚
Dental Pain Affects Learning
Children with untreated dental pain miss school, struggle to concentrate, and fall behind. Healthy teeth = better academic performance.
💪
Habits Formed Young Last a Lifetime
Children who learn proper oral care in school maintain better dental health throughout their adult lives.
